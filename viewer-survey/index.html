Context Discovery Surveys

Interactive surveys for gathering the inputs needed to build your context profiles. Each survey is designed as a focused, one-question-at-a-time experience that makes it easy to capture your authentic voice, audience insights, and business positioning.

Voice Discovery

Define your communication style, language patterns, and authentic voice characteristics.

19 questions ~10 min

ICP Discovery

Capture target market, decision makers, pains, desires, objections, and fit signals.

18 questions ~9 min

Business Profile

Document your offers, pricing, differentiation, and competitive positioning.

16 questions ~8 min

Claims Policy

Establish guardrails for what you can claim, banned absolutes, and safe language.

14 questions ~7 min

How It Works

One Question at a Time

Focus on each question without distraction. No overwhelming forms.

Progress Tracking

Always know exactly where you are and how much is left.

Auto-Save

Your answers are saved automatically. Resume anytime.

Export to JSON

Download or copy your answers for use with creator prompts.

Recommended Order

  1. Voice Discovery - Start here to define how you sound
  2. ICP Discovery - Then define who you're talking to
  3. Business Profile - Document what you offer and why
  4. Claims Policy - Establish guardrails for accuracy

Once completed, export your survey answers and use them with the corresponding creator prompts to generate your context profiles.
